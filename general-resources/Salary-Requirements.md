# Tech Career Salary & Requirements

**Notes on salary data:**

- Figures are 2026 US national averages/ranges aggregated from BLS, Glassdoor, ZipRecruiter, Salary.com, and PayScale
- Ranges reflect entry → senior unless otherwise noted
- Location matters significantly — tech hubs (SF, NYC, Seattle) pay 20–50% above national averages
- "Requirements" = what most employers actually list

## QUICK REFERENCE — Certifications That Move the Needle Most

|Cert|Roles It Affects|Salary Impact|
|---|---|---|
|CompTIA A+|Help desk, desktop support, IT technician|Entry-level baseline — gets you in the door|
|CompTIA Network+|Network admin, sysadmin, IT support|$5K–$15K boost at entry level|
|CompTIA Security+|All security roles, DoD contracts|11% average boost; appears in 70%+ of security postings|
|AWS SAA|Cloud, DevOps, DevSecOps, infrastructure|$15K–$25K; most widely requested cloud cert|
|AWS Security Specialty|Cloud security, DevSecOps|25% premium over base cloud roles|
|Terraform Associate|DevOps, DevSecOps, cloud, infrastructure|Meaningful differentiator at entry/mid level|
|CKA|Platform, DevOps, DevSecOps|High-signal; rare among entry-level candidates|
|OSCP|Penetration testing, red team|Highest salary correlation of any security cert ($117K–$151K avg posting)|
|CISSP|All senior security roles|22% average boost; requires 5 yrs experience to obtain|
|CCNA|Network admin, network engineer|$10K–$20K boost for networking-specific roles|
|CISA|Audit, compliance, GRC|Strong for IS audit and compliance-track roles|
|PMP|IT Project Manager, PM-adjacent roles|Standard expectation at mid-level PM roles|
