# Tech Certifications Guide — 2026

### Industry Recognized Certifications Across Every Major Tech Field

**How to use this guide:**
- Certifications are organized by field/category
- Within each category they are ranked **#1 being the most recognized and impactful** down to the least impactful
- Each cert includes: difficulty, exam cost, validity period, who it's for, and salary impact

---
## 1. IT Fundamentals & Support

_The entry point for most tech careers. These certs prove baseline competence and are recognized by 95%+ of HR departments as proof of IT fundamentals._

|Rank|Certification|Issuer|Difficulty|Cost|Valid|Best For|Salary Impact|
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
|🥇 1|**CompTIA A+**|CompTIA|Beginner|~$253 per exam (2 exams)|3 years|Anyone entering IT support, help desk, desktop support|$45K–$65K entry; 5–15% pay bump|
|🥈 2|**Google IT Support Certificate**|Google / Coursera|Beginner|~$49/month (~3–6 months)|No expiry|Career changers, no-experience entry into IT|$50K–$70K entry; 75% positive career outcomes|
|🥉 3|**CompTIA IT Fundamentals (ITF+)**|CompTIA|Beginner|~$127|3 years|Pre-A+, complete beginners, non-technical professionals exploring IT|Not a hiring cert — stepping stone to A+|

**Notes:**

- CompTIA A+ is recognized by 95% of HR teams as the baseline IT credential
- Google IT Support is backed by 150+ employers including Deloitte and Google itself
- ITF+ is not a hiring cert — it's a pre-requisite study tool before A+

## 2. Networking

_Networking knowledge underpins every other tech field. These certs prove you understand how data actually moves._

|Rank|Certification|Issuer|Difficulty|Cost|Valid|Best For|Salary Impact|
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
|🥇 1|**CCNA (200-301)**|Cisco|Intermediate|~$330|3 years|Network admins, sysadmins, anyone working with enterprise infrastructure|$75K–$105K; most recognized networking cert globally|
|🥈 2|**CompTIA Network+**|CompTIA|Beginner–Intermediate|~$349|3 years|Entry-level networking, stepping stone to CCNA or security certs|$60K–$85K; often listed alongside A+ in IT job posts|
|🥉 3|**CCNP Enterprise**|Cisco|Advanced|~$400 (core) + ~$300 (concentration)|3 years|Senior network engineers designing large-scale environments|$100K–$145K; significant step up from CCNA|
|4|**Juniper JNCIA-Junos**|Juniper|Beginner–Intermediate|~$200|3 years|Networking in telecom/SP environments using Juniper gear|$70K–$100K; less universal than Cisco|
|5|**CCIE (any track)**|Cisco|Expert|~$1,600 (lab) + written|3 years|Senior network architects and specialists|$125K–$200K; one of the most respected expert-level certs in IT|

**Notes:**

- CCNA is the most recognized networking cert globally and covers the most enterprise environments
- Network+ is more accessible than CCNA and vendor-neutral — good for IT generalists
- CCIE is one of the hardest certs in IT — roughly 1% of Cisco-certified professionals hold it
