**📌 Networking Resources**

**🆓 Free Resources**

🔗 **roadmap.sh — Networking Engineer** 
[link](https://roadmap.sh/network-engineer) — A good starting point if you don't know where to begin, or what order to learn things in.

🔗 **Cisco Networking Academy**
[link](https://www.netacad.com) — A comprehensive free networking resource. Covers Introduction to Networks, Networking Essentials, and more.

🔗 **Professor Messer — Network+ (YouTube)**  
[link](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=k7IOn3TiUc8&list=PLG49S3nxzAnl_tQe3kvnmeMid0mjF8Le8) — The same format as his Security+ series, and a gold standard for free prep for CompTIA Network+

🔗 **Microsoft Learn — Networking Fundamentals**  
[link](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/shows/networking-fundamentals/) — Free official Microsoft training that goes over protocols, TCP/IP, DNS, and how it maps to Azure networking specifically.


───────── **💰 PAID RESOURCES 💰** ─────────

🔗 **Google — The Bits and Bytes of Computer Networking**
[link](https://www.coursera.org/learn/computer-networking) — A well regarded beginner course covering how data actually moves across the internet.
